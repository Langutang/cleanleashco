What is Clean Leash

Clean Leash Company was born out of real-life, paws-on experience. Founded by a seasoned animal foster care expert who has cared for hundreds of pets, the idea sparked after countless outdoor adventures—walks, hikes, park days, and rescue missions—where she found herself wishing she could end those experiences feeling just as clean as they began.

Despite her best efforts, there was never a durable, convenient solution for keeping her hands sanitized on the go. This led to inspiration—and the creation of Clean Leash Company, a brand dedicated to designing durable, rugged sanitation tools made specifically for pet people on the move. The flagship product, Ruffresh, is the first of its kind: a compact, clip-on sanitization holder that attaches right to your leash for quick, reliable cleanliness wherever you and your four-legged companion roam.

What can Ruffresh do?

Ruffresh is the ultimate on-the-go sanitization solution for pet owners. Designed to clip securely onto any leash, Ruffresh is compact enough to stay out of the way, yet durable enough to withstand your daily adventures.

Whether you’re strolling through the park, hitting the trails, or just going around the block, Ruffresh keeps hand sanitizer or wipes easily accessible—so you can stay clean after handling waste, before grabbing snacks, or anytime you need a quick refresh. Built for convenience and crafted for the active pet parent, Ruffresh makes clean living a walk in the park
